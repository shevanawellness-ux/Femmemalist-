21 DAY HAIRCARE CHALLENGE

Day 1

  • Morning: Oil + Scalp Massage (10 min)
    Take a small amount of oil. Part your hair gently using fingers. Apply oil directly to the scalp (not just lengths).
    Use fingertips (not nails) and massage in slow circular motions across the entire scalp. Keep pressure light to moderate.
    Purpose: Improves blood circulation and nourishes roots.
  • Afternoon: Yoga – Downward Dog + Shoulder Stand (10 min)
    Hold each pose for 30–60 seconds. Keep breathing slow and controlled.
    Purpose: Improves blood flow to scalp and supports hair growth.
  • Evening: Acupressure + Sujok (5 min)
    Gently press fingertips and nail beds of each finger for 5–10 seconds.
    Rub nails of both hands together for 2–3 minutes (Balayam method).
    Purpose: Stimulates hair follicles through nerve activation.

Day 2

  • Morning: Rest (No oil today)
    Let scalp naturally absorb yesterday’s nourishment. Avoid over-touching hair.
  • Afternoon: Viparita Karani (10 min)
    Lie on your back with legs resting up against a wall. Keep body relaxed and breathe slowly.
    Purpose: Improves circulation to scalp without strain.
  • Evening: Inversion (2 min) + Gentle Combing
    Sit and bend forward slightly, letting head tilt down (do not strain).
    Use a wide-tooth comb and detangle slowly from ends to roots.
    Purpose: Prevents breakage and supports blood flow.

Day 3

  • Morning: Shampoo → Conditioner
    Wet hair completely. Take a small amount of shampoo and apply only to scalp.
    Massage gently, do not scratch. Rinse thoroughly.
    Apply conditioner only to mid-lengths and ends. Leave for 2–3 minutes, then rinse.
    Purpose: Cleans scalp without stripping natural oils.
  • Afternoon: Light Yoga (10 min)
    Include gentle stretches like neck rolls and cat-cow.
    Purpose: Releases tension that can affect scalp health.
  • Evening: Serum + Light Scalp Touch
    Apply 1–2 drops serum to damp or dry ends only.
    Lightly tap scalp with fingertips (no heavy massage).
    Purpose: Smoothens hair and maintains circulation.

Day 4

  • Morning: Hair Mask (20 min) → Rinse
    Apply mask to mid-lengths and ends. Avoid scalp unless instructed for your hair type.
    Leave for 15–20 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with water.
    Purpose: Deep nourishment and repair.
  • Afternoon: Light Oil Application (Optional)
    If scalp feels dry, apply a very small amount of oil only to dry areas.
    Skip if scalp feels balanced.
  • Evening: Acupressure + Sujok (5 min)
    Repeat nail rubbing and fingertip pressure technique.
    Purpose: Continues follicle stimulation.

Day 5

  • Morning: Rest Day
    No oil, no wash. Let scalp rebalance naturally.
  • Afternoon: Yoga (10 min)
    Include forward bends and gentle inversions.
    Purpose: Maintains circulation and reduces stress.
  • Evening: Leave-in Serum
    Apply very small amount to ends only.
    Use fingers to gently smooth hair, avoid roots.
    Purpose: Prevents dryness and adds shine.

Day 6

  • Morning: Rest

  • Afternoon: Meditation + Inversion (5–7 min)
    Sit comfortably, close your eyes, and take slow deep breaths.

    Purpose: Reduces stress, which directly impacts hair fall.
  • Evening: Scalp Massage (Dry – 5 to 7 min)
    Use fingertips and gently massage scalp in circular motions without oil.

    Purpose: Improves circulation without overloading scalp.
